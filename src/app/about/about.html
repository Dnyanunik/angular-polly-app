About Our Project

Serverless Text-to-Speech Accessibility Tool designed to convert written content into lifelike speech for people with visual or reading difficulties.


Problem

Web content is inaccessible to visually impaired users.

Solution

Real-time serverless text-to-speech conversion.

Cloud Stack

AWS Polly, Lambda, S3 or Azure Speech & Functions.

Future Scope

Emotional AI powered real-time speech synthesis.
